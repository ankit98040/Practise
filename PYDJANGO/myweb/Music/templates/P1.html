COVID 19

COVID-19 (coronavirus disease 2019) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2), previously known as the 2019 novel coronavirus (2019-nCoV), a strain of coronavirus. The first cases were seen in Wuhan, China, in late December 2019 before spreading globally 1,2,10. The current outbreak was officially recognized as a pandemic on 11 March 2020 44.Current imaging best practice advises that CT chest is not used to diagnose COVID-19, but maybe helpful in assessing for complications 115. The non-specific imaging findings are most commonly of atypical or organizing pneumonia, often with a bilateral, peripheral, and basal predominant distribution 32. No effective treatment or vaccine exists currently (April 2020) 20.
